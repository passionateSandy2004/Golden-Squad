Field Of Focus

In our Unity 3D training, we'll start with the basics of 3D game development, covering the Unity interface, game objects, and scene creation. You'll master C# scripting to build interactive game mechanics and develop efficient, well-organized code. We'll also delve into augmented reality development using Unity's AR Foundation, learning to create immersive AR applications. By the end, you'll be equipped to build both 3D games and AR experiences.

In our web development training, we'll begin with the basics of HTML and CSS, including Tailwind CSS, to create structured and styled web pages. You'll learn JavaScript for interactive and dynamic content. We'll cover various aspects of web development, including responsive design, web frameworks, and best practices. By the end, you'll be equipped to build modern, responsive websites with a strong foundation in front-end development.

In our AI with Python training, we'll start with Python basics and move on to machine learning techniques like supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning. We'll delve into neural networks, their structure, and functionality, and you'll build and train them using TensorFlow and PyTorch. We'll also use OpenCV for real-world AI projects, such as image recognition and object detection. By the end, you'll have a solid foundation in AI and machine learning, ready to tackle practical problems with advanced techniques.

Training Process

Our training process for trainees is meticulously structured to ensure comprehensive learning and skill development. We employ a series of well-defined stages that include theoretical instruction, practical exercises, and real-world simulations.

1.

Basics and Intermediate
(3rd Sem)

1. Learn Basics: Access curated YouTube tutorials, certified courses, and online resources to build a solid foundation.
2. Mini Projects: Apply your knowledge through practical mini projects with mentor guidance.
3. Build Portfolio: Showcase your skills with completed projects.
4. Prepare for Internships: Gain industry connections and be ready for internship opportunities.

2.

Internships and Advacement
(3rd Sem End)

Leveraging your skills and knowledge, we will facilitate opportunities for you to secure internships at startups, enabling you to gain valuable industry-level experience and practical insights that will enhance your professional growth and readiness for the tech industry.

3.

Industrial Projects
(4th Sem)

As a team, we engage in real-time industrial projects, including those sourced from dynamic startups. By applying our collective expertise, we deliver high-quality solutions while gaining invaluable practical experience and insights into real-world applications, further enhancing our professional development.

4.

Direct Client Projects
(5th Sem)

We secure direct projects from clients, ensuring a steady flow of real-world challenges. Additionally, our MBA students source projects from various sectors of the business world, providing us with a diverse range of opportunities to tackle and excel in. This comprehensive approach enriches our experience and positions us at the forefront of industry innovation.(Freelancing)

By the end of the 3rd year, students are fully industry-ready, having gained substantial internship and real-world experience
that positions them as valuable assets in the professional landscape.
